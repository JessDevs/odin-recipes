Caesar Salad

Return to main recipe page

Description

This is a homemade Caesar Salad recipe using Romaine Lettuce, cheese, and of course, croutons. Using some Garlic, dijon, lemon juice, vinegar etc we will be making the salad dressing ourselves!

Ingredients:

  • 2 Romaine Lettuce heads
  • Parmesan Cheese, shredded
  • Crispy Croutons/li>
  • Salad Dressing (more on that later)
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Worcestershire
  • Lemon Juice
  • Red Wine vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/8 tsp black pepper

Steps:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine all ingredients
  2. Toss gently to coat the lettuce
  3. Vwala! Simple!
