Description

There is nothing quite like an original Taco (using homemade mix). This recipe will use lean ground beef so that there is no need to drain any fat. It will also use a hard, or soft tortilla shell!

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb lean ground beef
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • 12 taco shells
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

Steps:

  1. Break apart lean ground beef in skillet, medium heat
  2. Add chili powder, cumin, salt, oregano, garlic powder, and pepper.
  3. Cook until beef is browned
  4. Add tomato sauce and water to the meat
  5. Cook until liquid has evaporated and meat is saucy
  6. 2 tablespoons of taco meat into hard or soft shell tortilla
  7. Top with your favorite toppings!
