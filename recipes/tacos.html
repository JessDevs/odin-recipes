The Classic Tacos

Description

There is nothing quite like an original Taco (using homemade mix). This recipe will use lean ground beef so that there is no need to drain any fat. It will also use a hard, or soft tortilla shell!

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb lean ground beef
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • 12 taco shells
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
